# Pitfalls ⚠️

-   Hidden assumptions in environment or calibration setup that can
    compromise test validity.
-   Missing negative-path scenarios for high-criticality behavior, which
    may overlook potential failure modes.
-   Incomplete traceability from requirement to verdict, hindering
    accountability and compliance.
