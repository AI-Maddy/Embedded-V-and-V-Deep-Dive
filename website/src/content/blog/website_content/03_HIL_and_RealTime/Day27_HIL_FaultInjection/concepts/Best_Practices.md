# ✅ Best Practices

-   **Concise Concept Notes**: Keep concept notes short, testable, and
    directly linked to requirements for clarity. This enhances
    understanding and reduces ambiguity.
-   **Confidence Levels**: Record confidence levels and known
    limitations to provide context for findings. This transparency is
    essential for informed decision-making.
-   **Consistent Naming**: Use consistent naming conventions for
    artifacts and verdicts to avoid confusion. Consistency aids in
    clarity and communication.
