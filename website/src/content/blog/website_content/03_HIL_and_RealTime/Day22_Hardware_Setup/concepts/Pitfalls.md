# ⚠️ Pitfalls

-   **Hidden Drift**: Be cautious of hidden model/configuration drift
    across runs, which can skew results.
-   **Mixing Intent with Implementation**: Keep requirement intent
    separate from implementation details to maintain clarity.
-   **Traceability Gaps**: Ensure that traceability links in review
    notes are complete and accurate.
