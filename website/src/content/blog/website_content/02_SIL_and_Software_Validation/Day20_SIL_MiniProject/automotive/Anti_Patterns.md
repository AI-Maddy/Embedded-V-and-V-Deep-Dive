# Anti-Patterns 🚫

-   Generic test claims without domain hazard mapping lead to
    insufficient safety validation.
-   Pass/fail decisions without quantitative thresholds can result in
    ambiguous outcomes.
-   Evidence summaries without raw artifact references diminish the
    reliability of the validation process.
