# 🔎 Pre-Review Checklist 📝

☐ Boundaries are clear and well-defined. ☐ Failure criteria are explicit
and measurable. ☐ Evidence hooks are identified and traceable. ☐
Reviewer can reproduce the reasoning and results. ☐ All assumptions are
documented and validated.
