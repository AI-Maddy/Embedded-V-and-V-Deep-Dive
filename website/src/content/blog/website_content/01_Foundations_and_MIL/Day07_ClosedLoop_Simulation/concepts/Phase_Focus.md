# Phase Focus 🌟

This day emphasizes: - **Model Fidelity**: Ensure the simulation
accurately represents the system. - **Requirement Intent Clarity**:
Confirm that requirements are unambiguous and testable. - **Simulation
Confidence**: Validate that the simulation results are reliable and
reproducible.
