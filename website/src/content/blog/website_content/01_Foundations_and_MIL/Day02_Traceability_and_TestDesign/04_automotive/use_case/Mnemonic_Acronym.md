# Mnemonic Acronym 🚦

**TRACE**: Testable Requirements Aligned with Critical Evidence - **T**:
Traceability from requirement to test artifact. - **R**: Robustness
under nominal, boundary, and fault conditions. - **A**: Alignment with
domain-specific standards. - **C**: Coverage of all critical hazards and
interfaces. - **E**: Evidence-based validation and verification.
