# 🔎 Pre-Review Checklist ✅

☐ Concept definitions are precise, testable, and aligned with standards.
☐ Assumptions and limits are documented and reviewed. ☐ Verification
signals and metrics are identified and measurable. ☐ Evidence references
are present, reproducible, and traceable. ☐ Model configurations are
version-controlled and validated. ☐ Boundary conditions and failure
modes are explicitly tested.
