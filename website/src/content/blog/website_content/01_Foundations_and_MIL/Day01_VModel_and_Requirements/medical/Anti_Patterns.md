# Anti-Patterns 🚫

-   Generic test claims without domain hazard mapping
-   Pass/fail decisions without quantitative thresholds
-   Evidence summaries without raw artifact references

::: warning
::: title
Warning
:::

Avoid anti-patterns as they compromise compliance and patient safety.
**ISO 14971** emphasizes the importance of hazard mapping and risk
quantification.
