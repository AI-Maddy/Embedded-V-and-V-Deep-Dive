# Core Concept 🔑

-   **Define** the primary mechanism and expected behavior of the
    software under test.
-   **Identify** assumptions and boundary conditions that may affect
    performance.
-   **State** what failure looks like and how it should be detected,
    ensuring clarity in the criteria for success and failure.
