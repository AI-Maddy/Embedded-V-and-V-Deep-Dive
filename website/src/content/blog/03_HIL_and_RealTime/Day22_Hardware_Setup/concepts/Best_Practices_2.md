# ✅ Best Practices

-   **Concise and Testable Notes**: Keep concept notes short, testable,
    and linked to requirements for clarity.
-   **Confidence Levels**: Record confidence levels and known
    limitations for each concept.
-   **Consistent Naming**: Use consistent naming for artifacts and
    verdicts to avoid confusion.
