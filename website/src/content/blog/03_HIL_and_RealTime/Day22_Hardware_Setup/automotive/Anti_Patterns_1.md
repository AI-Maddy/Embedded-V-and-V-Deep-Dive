# Anti-Patterns ❌

-   Generic test claims without domain hazard mapping, which can lead to
    unaddressed risks.
-   Pass/fail decisions without quantitative thresholds, making it
    difficult to assess true performance.
-   Evidence summaries without raw artifact references, leading to gaps
    in traceability and accountability.
