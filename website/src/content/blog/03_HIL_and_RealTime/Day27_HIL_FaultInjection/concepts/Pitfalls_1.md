# ⚠️ Pitfalls

-   **Hidden Drift**: Be wary of hidden model/configuration drift across
    runs, which can skew results. Regular checks should be implemented
    to identify any discrepancies.
-   **Mixing Intent with Implementation**: Keep requirement intent
    separate from implementation details to maintain clarity. This
    separation aids in clear communication and understanding.
-   **Missing Traceability Links**: Ensure traceability links in review
    notes are present to facilitate thorough evaluations. Traceability
    is key for compliance with standards.
