# 🚫 Anti-Patterns ⚠️

\### 📝 Relying, Ignoring, Recording 📝

-   **Relying** on intuition without artifact evidence.
-   **Ignoring** coupling between interfaces and timing.
-   **Recording** outcomes without requirement references, leading to
    ambiguity.
