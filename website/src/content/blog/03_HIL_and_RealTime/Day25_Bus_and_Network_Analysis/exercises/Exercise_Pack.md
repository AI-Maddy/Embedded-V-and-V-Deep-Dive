# 📋 Exercise Pack 📦

1.  **Nominal Scenario**: A scenario with explicit pass/fail criteria
    that represents expected behavior under normal conditions.
2.  **Boundary Scenario**: A scenario that tests the limits of timing,
    value, or mode transitions to ensure robustness.
3.  **Fault/Negative Scenario**: A scenario designed to provoke errors,
    with expected detection and recovery behavior.
4.  **Rerun Consistency Check**: A task to verify repeatability of
    results across multiple executions.
