# Core Concept 🧩

-   **Define** the primary mechanism and expected behavior of the system
    under test.
-   **Identify** assumptions and boundary conditions that may impact
    system performance.
-   **State** what failure looks like and how it should be detected,
    ensuring clarity in expectations.
