# 🚫 Anti-Patterns ⚠️

-   **Relying** on intuition without artifact evidence can lead to
    critical oversights.
-   **Ignoring** coupling between interfaces and timing can result in
    unpredictable behavior.
-   **Recording** outcomes without requirement references diminishes the
    reliability of the findings.
