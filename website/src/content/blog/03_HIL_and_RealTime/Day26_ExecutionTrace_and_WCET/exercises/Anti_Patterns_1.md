# 🚫 Anti-Patterns ❌

-   Tuning thresholds after seeing failing results can lead to biased
    outcomes.
-   Mixing multiple uncontrolled changes in one run complicates
    analysis.
-   Summarizing outcomes without raw evidence pointers undermines
    traceability.
