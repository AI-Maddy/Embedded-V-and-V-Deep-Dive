::: admonition
🚀 Remember

Fault injection is not about proving the system **works** --- nominal
tests do that. Fault injection is about proving the system **fails
safely**. Every hazard in the FHA is a question: \"What happens when
this goes wrong?\" Your fault injection campaign is the answer --- and
the answer must be documented, quantified, and traceable all the way
back to the safety assessment.
:::
