# 🧭 Patterns 🧭

-   **Invariants First**: Define invariants before deriving measurable
    checks.
-   **Explicit Contracts**: Keep interface contracts clear (units,
    ranges, rates).
-   **Evidence Mapping**: Map each concept to at least one evidence
    artifact.
