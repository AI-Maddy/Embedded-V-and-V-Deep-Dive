# Pre-Review Checklist ✅

Use this checklist to ensure readiness for review:

☐ Requirements are fully traceable to tests and artifacts. ☐ All tests
have measurable pass/fail criteria. ☐ Nominal, boundary, and fault
scenarios are included for each requirement. ☐ Residual risks are
documented with ownership and next actions. ☐ Evidence aligns with
domain standards (e.g., **DO-178C**, **ARP4761**).
