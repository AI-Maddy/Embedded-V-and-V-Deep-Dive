# 🌈 TRACE Mnemonic 🌈

To ensure comprehensive traceability in MIL testing, remember
**TRACE**: - **T**: Timestamped logging for evidence - **R**:
Requirement-driven pass/fail criteria - **A**: Artifact capture for
audit trails - **C**: Clear classification of results (Nominal 🟢,
Boundary 🟡, Fault 🔴) - **E**: Evidence-based reporting and risk
documentation
