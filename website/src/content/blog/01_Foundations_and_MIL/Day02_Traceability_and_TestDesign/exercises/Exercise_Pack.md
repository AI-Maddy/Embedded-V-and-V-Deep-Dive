# 📋 Exercise Pack 📋

1.  🟢 **Nominal Scenario**: Explicit pass/fail criteria tied to
    requirement IDs.
2.  🟡 **Boundary Scenario**: Near limits (timing, value, or mode
    transitions) with stressed comparison artifacts.
3.  🔴 **Fault/Negative Scenario**: Expected detection and recovery
    behavior with mitigation evidence.
4.  🟢 **Rerun Consistency Check**: Repeatability validation across
    multiple runs.

::: admonition
Example Expansion Include one nominal, one boundary, and one fault
scenario per objective to meet **ARP4754A Section 5.2** coverage
expectations.
:::
