🚀 Remember

In aerospace domain modeling, **the pattern IS the argument**. Choosing
the right pattern --- hierarchical state machine for mode logic,
explicit SSM decoding for bus interfaces, independent monitors for
cross-channel comparison --- tells the DER exactly how your design
handles failures before they reach the hardware. Document your pattern
choices; they are certification evidence.
