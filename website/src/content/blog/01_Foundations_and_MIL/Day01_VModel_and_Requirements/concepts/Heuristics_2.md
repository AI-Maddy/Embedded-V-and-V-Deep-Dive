# 🧪 Heuristics 🧪

-   **If it cannot be measured, it is not yet review-ready**: Focus on
    measurable outcomes for all verification steps.
-   **If two reviewers interpret differently, refine wording**: Ensure
    clarity and consensus in requirement definitions.
-   **If a failure is possible, define detection evidence**: Example:
    \"Fault X triggers alarm Y.\"
