# 🛑 Anti-Patterns 🛑

-   **Theory without Measurable Criteria**: Avoid abstract explanations
    that lack acceptance criteria.
-   **Ignoring Boundary Behavior**: Treat boundary conditions as
    mandatory, not optional.
-   **Undocumented Assumptions**: Do not use assumptions that are not
    explicitly recorded.
