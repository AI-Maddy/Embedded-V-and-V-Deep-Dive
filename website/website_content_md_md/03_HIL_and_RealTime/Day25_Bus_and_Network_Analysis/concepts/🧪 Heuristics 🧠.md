# 🧪 Heuristics 🧠

\### 📝 What Must, What Fails, What Artifact 📝

-   **What must** always remain true? (e.g., bus communication must not
    exceed defined latency)
-   **What fails** first near limits? (e.g., signal integrity under high
    load)
-   **What artifact** proves the verdict? (e.g., logs showing successful
    communication)
