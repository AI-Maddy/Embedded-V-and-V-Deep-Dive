# ✅ Best Practices

-   Keep rerun steps deterministic. .. note:: Deterministic steps ensure
    repeatability and compliance with **DO-178C**.
-   Store artifacts with version/time metadata. .. admonition:: Example
    Use a structured naming convention:
    [\<TestID\>\_\<Timestamp\>\_\<Version\>]{.title-ref}.
-   Review findings with risk owner before closure. .. important:: Risk
    ownership ensures accountability and proper mitigation.
