# 🔎 Checklist 📋

☐ Pass/fail thresholds are unambiguous. ☐ Nominal + stress + fault
evidence is present. ☐ Traceability and residual risk are documented. ☐
Configuration snapshots are captured for each run. ☐ Environment/version
metadata is logged. ☐ Residual risks are assigned ownership and next
actions.

::: {.admonition .hint}
Pre-Review Checklist

Before review, ensure: - All artifacts are traceable to requirements. -
Evidence supports deterministic behavior. - Residual risks are clearly
documented.
:::
