🔄 **Closed-Loop Day** --- Today we close the feedback loop:
**controller ↔ plant ↔ sensors ↔ bus ↔ controller**. Open-loop MIL (Day
06) verified individual command responses; closed-loop MIL verifies that
the **entire flight-control system** behaves correctly when all elements
interact simultaneously --- including emergent dynamics that only appear
when the loop is closed.
