⚙️ **Automation Day** --- Manual MIL execution does not scale. A DAL-A
flight control program typically has 200--500 MIL scenarios across
nominal, boundary, and fault categories. Running them by hand introduces
human error, breaks reproducibility, and blocks regression. Today we
build the **automation pipeline** that makes every MIL run scripted,
repeatable, hash-verified, and evidence-packaged --- ready for DER audit
without a single manual click.
:::
