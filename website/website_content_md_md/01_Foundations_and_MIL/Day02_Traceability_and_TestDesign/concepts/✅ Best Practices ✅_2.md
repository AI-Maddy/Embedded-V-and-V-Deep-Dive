# ✅ Best Practices ✅

-   **Concise Notes**: Keep concept notes short, testable, and linked to
    requirements.
-   **Confidence Levels**: Record confidence levels and known
    limitations.
-   **Consistent Naming**: Use consistent naming conventions for
    artifacts and verdicts.

::: admonition
**Standards Reminder** - **DO-254 Section 6.2**: Ensure
hardware-software integration testing aligns with traceability goals. -
**IEC 62304 Clause 5.7**: Verify software system design against safety
requirements.
:::
