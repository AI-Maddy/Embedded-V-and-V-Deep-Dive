📌 Phase Context: MIL 🛠️
-------------------------
This day emphasizes **model behavior realism**, **requirement intent**, and **early defect discovery**. MIL (Model-in-the-Loop) is the foundation for verifying embedded systems models before hardware integration, ensuring alignment with standards like **DO-178C**, **ISO 26262**, and **ARP4754A**. 

.. important::  
   Understanding the context of MIL is essential for effective V&V practices. It allows teams to identify potential issues early, reducing costs and improving safety.
