📚 Mnemonic Acronym: "SMART-V" 🌟
---------------------------------
**S** - Scenario Matrix for coverage  
**M** - Metrics for correctness, timing, and robustness  
**A** - Artifacts linked to requirements  
**R** - Residual risks triaged  
**T** - Traceability for verdicts  
**V** - Validation of evidence completeness
