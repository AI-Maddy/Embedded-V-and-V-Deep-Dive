Validation Focus 🔍
-------------------
- **Numerical Stability** 🟢: Ensure stability under expected operating envelopes.  
  - Standards Reference: *IEC 62304: Medical Device Software Stability*.  

- **Sensitivity Analysis** 🟡: Evaluate response to disturbances and parameter variation.  
  - Standards Reference: *ISO 26262-9: Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment*.  

- **Mode Transitions** 🔴: Verify correct fallback behavior under fault conditions.  
  - Standards Reference: *DO-254: Design Assurance Guidance for Hardware*.
