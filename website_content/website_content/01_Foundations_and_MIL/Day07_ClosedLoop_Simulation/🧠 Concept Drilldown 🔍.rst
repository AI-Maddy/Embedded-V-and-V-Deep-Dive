🧠 Concept Drilldown 🔍
-----------------------
- **Primary Mechanism**: Identify the signal, state, or computation governing expected behavior.  
- **Boundary Conditions**: Define where nominal assumptions start to break.  
- **Safety Impact**: Assess how failure propagates into system-level risk.  
- **Verification Hook**: Pinpoint observable artifacts that prove correctness.

.. important::
   Early detection of defects in MIL reduces downstream integration and system-level risks, aligning with **ARP4754A Section 5.3** for early validation strategies.
