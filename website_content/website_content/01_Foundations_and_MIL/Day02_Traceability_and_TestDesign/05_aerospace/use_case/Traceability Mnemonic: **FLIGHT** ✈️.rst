Traceability Mnemonic: **FLIGHT** ✈️
------------------------------------
**F** - Functional correctness.  
**L** - Linkage to requirements.  
**I** - Interface constraints verified.  
**G** - Guidance from domain standards (DO-178C, DO-254, ARP4754A/ARP4761).  
**H** - Hazard analysis and mitigation.  
**T** - Timing and robustness checks.
