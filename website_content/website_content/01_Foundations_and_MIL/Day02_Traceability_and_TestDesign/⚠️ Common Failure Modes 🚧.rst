⚠️ Common Failure Modes 🚧
--------------------------
- **Ambiguous Acceptance Criteria**: Leads to inconsistent test results.  
- **Hidden Model Drift**: Configuration changes unnoticed between runs.  
- **Overlooked Recovery Paths**: Degraded-mode checks often skipped.  
- **Incomplete Artifact Management**: Missing naming/versioning conventions.  

.. warning::  
   Hidden assumptions in MIL tests can lead to **system-level failures** during later phases. Ensure **IEC 62304 compliance** for medical devices and **ISO 26262 Part 9** for automotive safety.
