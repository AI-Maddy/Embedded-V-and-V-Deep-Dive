✅ Required Deliverables
------------------------
- **Scenario Matrix**: Map objectives to scenarios clearly. This matrix should be comprehensive and easy to understand.
- **Raw Logs/Traces/Plots**: Include timestamps for all artifacts collected. Accurate logging is crucial for post-test analysis.
- **Requirement-Linked Verdict Summary**: Summarize findings with direct links to requirements. This summary provides a clear overview of compliance status.
- **Residual-Risk and Next-Action List**: Identify unresolved issues and their ownership. This list is essential for ensuring that all risks are managed appropriately.
