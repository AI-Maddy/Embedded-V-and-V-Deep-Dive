# List of Severity Levels 🟢🟡🔴

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Severity     **Description**
  Level**        
  -------------- --------------------------------------------------------
  🟢 Nominal     Expected behavior under normal operating conditions.

  🟡 Boundary    Behavior near thresholds or limits, requiring close
                 monitoring.

  🔴 Fault       Erroneous or unsafe behavior requiring immediate
                 intervention.
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

::: admonition
