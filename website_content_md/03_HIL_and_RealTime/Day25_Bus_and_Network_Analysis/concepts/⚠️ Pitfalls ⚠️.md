# ⚠️ Pitfalls ⚠️

\### 📝 Hidden Assumptions, Boundary Behavior, Traceability Gaps 📝

-   **Hidden assumptions** reducing reproducibility.
-   **Boundary behavior** left uncharacterized, risking undetected
    failures.
-   **Traceability gaps** between concept and test, complicating
    validation efforts.
