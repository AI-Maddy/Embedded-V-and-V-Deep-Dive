# 🚫 Anti-Patterns

-   **Theory without Criteria**: Avoid explaining theory without
    measurable acceptance criteria. This can lead to subjective
    interpretations of test results.
-   **Boundary Behavior Ignorance**: Treating boundary behavior as
    optional can lead to undetected issues. Always include boundary
    testing in your HIL strategy.
-   **Undocumented Assumptions**: Using undocumented assumptions during
    analysis can compromise the validity of the results. Ensure all
    assumptions are recorded and reviewed.
