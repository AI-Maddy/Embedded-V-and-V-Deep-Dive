# 🔹 **Best Practices** 🔹

\### 🟢 **Artifact Tagging** 📝

Tag every artifact with domain requirement IDs to maintain traceability
and ensure compliance with safety standards.

\### 🟡 **Timing and Functional Evidence** 📊

Capture timing and functional evidence in the same run package to ensure
comprehensive testing and analysis.

\### 🟢 **Residual Risk and Ownership** 📝

Record residual risk and ownership before closure to ensure that
potential safety issues are addressed and mitigated.
