# 🚫 Anti-Patterns 🚫

-   **Uncontrolled Configurations**: Running tests without fixed
    snapshots.
-   **Subjective Criteria**: Declaring pass/fail without quantitative
    thresholds.
-   **Incomplete Evidence**: Logging summaries without raw artifact
    references.

::: warning
::: title
Warning
:::

Anti-patterns can lead to non-compliance with **DO-254 Section 5.2** and
**IEC 62304 Section 5.3**.
:::
